FAQ

We already have a CRM. Is the sprint still useful?

Yes. Most projects start with a CRM nobody trusts. The tool is rarely the problem. I work with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive and even Excel.

How much of my team's time does it take?

Roughly 2–4 hours a week from the sales lead, plus one team workshop per week. I do the building. Your team checks it against reality and starts using it.

What exactly do we have at the end of 2 weeks?

A configured pipeline, a live dashboard, an outbound cadence and a weekly review routine, set up in your own tools and documented so your team can run it without me.

What does "from 2'500 CHF" depend on?

Both. I'm based in Switzerland and work remotely with companies worldwide, including the US. On-site days are possible when it makes sense.
